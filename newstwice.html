TWICE Becomes 1st K-Pop Girl Group To Spend 3 Weeks In Top 100 Of Billboard 200 With An EP

Jul 6 - 2021

TWICE continues to make Billboard history with their latest mini album! Last month, TWICE’s “Taste of Love” became the first mini album (EP) by a female K-pop act ever to enter the top 10 of the Billboard 200 (Billboard’s weekly ranking of the most popular albums in the United States), making a historic debut at No. 6. As it spends a third consecutive week on the chart, “Taste of Love” has now also become the first mini album by any K-pop girl group ever to spend three weeks in the top 100 of the Billboard 200.

TWICE Makes Billboard History As 1st Girl Group (From Any Country) To Hit No. 1 On Top Album Sales Chart With An EP

Jun 22 - 2021

TWICE has achieved a historic first on the Billboard charts! For the week ending on June 26, TWICE’s new mini album “Taste of Love” debuted at No. 1 on three separate Billboard charts. “Taste of Love” entered Billboard’s Top Album Sales chart, Top Current Album Sales chart, and World Albums chart at No. 1. With this latest achievement, TWICE has become the first girl group in history—not just K-pop—ever to top Billboard’s Top Album Sales chart with an EP. Only five other girl groups have ever topped the chart with any album at all: Destiny’s Child, TLC, Spice Girls, Danity Kane, and BLACKPINK.
