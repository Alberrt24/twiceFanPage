&TWICE mini ALBUM

&TWICE is the second full Japanese album by South Korean girl group Twice.
The album was released digitally and physically on November 20, 2019 by Warner Music Japan.
The album consists of 10 tracks including the new title track Fake & True as well as Happy Happy and Breakthrough.
A repackage featuring the new song Swing was released on February 5, 2020.
On September 6, 2019 it was announced on the official Twice Japan website that Twice would be releasing their second Japanese Album.
